Captain America for president

Biography

Captain America, also known as Steve Rogers, is a World War II veteran and a symbol of courage, integrity, and justice. Originally a frail young man, he was transformed into a super-soldier through a government experiment to fight tyranny and defend freedom. Throughout his life, Captain America has stood for the values of equality, honor, and perseverance, becoming a leader both on and off the battlefield.
