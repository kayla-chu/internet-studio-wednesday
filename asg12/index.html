My Top 12 Favorite Movies

These movies have stayed with me for their unforgettable stories, inspiring characters, and emotional depth. Whether they make me laugh, cry, or dream big, each film on this list reflects something I love about storytelling and imagination.

Pilgrim's Progress

A powerful animated story about faith, perseverance, and redemption.

Avengers: Endgame

A monumental superhero film bringing closure to over a decade of storytelling.

Parent Trap

A heartwarming and funny story about twin sisters reuniting their parents.

Thor: Ragnarok

A colorful, action-packed adventure with humor and heart.

Finding Nemo

A touching underwater journey about family, courage, and never giving up.

The Incredibles

Superhero family teamwork meets heartfelt storytelling and humor.

Big Hero 6

A fun, futuristic tale of friendship, grief, and bravery.

Fast & Furious 7

High-speed action with themes of loyalty, family, and farewell.

Spider-Man: Far From Home

An exciting global adventure that explores responsibility and identity.

Cinderella

A timeless fairy tale about kindness, courage, and believing in magic.

The Lion King

A visually stunning and emotional story about destiny and leadership.

Mean Girls

A sharp, funny, and iconic look at friendship and high school dynamics.
